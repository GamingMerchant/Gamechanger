Introduction

Welcome to RetroArcade - Pi Network Edition ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy will inform you about how we look after your personal data when you visit our website and use our services, and tell you about your privacy rights.

When you use RetroArcade - Pi Network Edition, we may collect the following types of information:

Information Provided by Pi Network

  • Pi Network username
  • Pi Network user ID
  • Authentication tokens

Game-Related Information

  • Game scores and progress

Technical Information

We use your data for the following purposes:

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your data with:

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

Our services are not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
